About this property

Juneau Vacation Home: Stunning View + Beach Access

Escape to the mountains and experience the great outdoors at this lovely Juneau vacation rental! Perched on the shore of Lena Cove, this 2-bedroom, 1-bath home is the perfect gateway for those seeking to enjoy beaches and local restaurants or hiking nearby trails. After your outdoor adventures, kick back on the private deck and admire the breathtaking panoramic views!

-- THE PROPERTY --

CB10000104 | 185 Sq Ft | 2 Private Decks | Lena Cove & Mountain Views | 2 Bicycles Provided
